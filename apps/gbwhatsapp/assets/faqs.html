1. Why GBWhatsApp Pro not found in Play Store?

GBWhatsApp Pro is developed by a 3rd party developer. Unfortunately, due to copyright and license problems, it isn’t available on PlayStore.

2. Can I keep both WhatsApp and GBWhatsApp Pro both installed together?

No, you cannot keep both WhatsApp and GBWhatsApp Pro installed together on the same device.

3. How do I import conversations from WhatsApp to GBWhatsApp Pro?

  1. Backup your conversations on WhatsApp. (In WhatsApp settings, go to ‘Chat settings’ and backup your conversations’)
  2. Install GBWhatsApp Pro (follow steps from question 3 above).
  3. After verification is complete you will be prompted to import the backup.
  4. Accept the request to import conversations.
